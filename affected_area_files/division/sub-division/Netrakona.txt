At least five people, including four members of a family, were killed and 25 people injured as a nor'wester lashed Kalmakanda upazila on Sunday night.

The deceased were identified as Afroza Begum, 26, and her three sons Rony, 2, Pervez, 9, Rana, 5,of Bisompur village, and Mamun, 25, son of Alauddin of Noyanagar village in the upazila.

 

Locals said they died as their thatched house collapsed on them during the storm, leaving four of them dead.
